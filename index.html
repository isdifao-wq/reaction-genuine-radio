HOW TO GO LIVE

Step-by-step guide for Prod Difao to publish RGR online and control programming in real time.

1

Create Your Station on Zeno.fm (Free)

Go to zeno.fm → Create Account → New Station → Name it "Reaction Genuine Radio". You get a public stream URL and an embeddable player instantly. Free tier available.

2

Upload Your Beats & Songs

In Zeno's dashboard, upload your MP3/WAV files. Create playlists per show (Morning Beat Session, Late Night Vibes, etc.). Set each playlist to auto-schedule at the right times.

3

Go Live With Your Mic (Exclusive Drops)

Download BUTT (Broadcast Using This Tool — free) or Mixxx (free DJ software). Enter your Zeno server details — host, port, password — and you can broadcast your voice live, drop exclusives in real time, or mix tracks live.

4

Upgrade to Radio.co for Full Control ($49/mo)

Radio.co gives you a drag-and-drop scheduler, live show management, listener analytics, and a professional player. You can see exactly who's listening and switch shows from a dashboard — like a real radio station.

5

Connect Your Stream to This Website

Once you have your Zeno/Radio.co stream URL (format: https://stream.zeno.fm/yourstation), send it to me and I'll embed a live working player directly into this site so visitors hear RGR the moment they arrive.
